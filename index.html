Spanish has played an important role in her life. Not only was it the first language she was taught by her parents but it was the first one she mastered at a young age. This sparked her independence since she was not able to get full support from her family, and she had to find her own learning techniques. Although she mastered English, she did not stop speaking Spanish nor did it prevent her from communicating with her family. She saw English as a useful tool as she began utilizing this tool to translate letters and news to her parents. This tactic allowed both her and her parents to have an interaction in society and not hide behind the shadows. Ultimately, this notion of helping others who might be experiencing the same struggles influenced her to pursue her career in the Spanish field
